The Back Road

The Philosphy

So in case you were wondering what this is all about ...

The Back Road is a group of cooperative,
skilled CoC players.

There are quite a few skilled clans in the Clash of Clans, but most of them have a highly competitive environment: the pressure to perform is huge, which in turn creates a toxic environment.


The Back Road is not that clan. We are built in a vision of cooperation, where failure is an option, and is even encouraged: failure is the best way to learn, if you are willing to learn from mistakes.


This clan is built by a group of people that came from top ranking war clans that were looking for a more cooperative environment. We created this environment to invite you to join us, learn from your mistakes and grow to the next level.

The Rules

Communicate

It might seem simple, but it is important: talk, talk, talk. We are a clan that would like to help each other, but if you're not open for conversation, there is not much we can do to help.

Be kind

There are actual people with feelings at the other end of the connection. Be mindful of this, and don't talk to someone in a way you wouldn't talk to them in real life.

Promotions

Normally in clans, promotions are an honour only and don't have much value. In the Back Road, however, this status has actual meaning: anyone with elder status is required to respond to requests for feedback from other members.


This gives our clan a clear structure. If you don't have the time to go through someone's attack and give him/her advice, don't take an elder position.

Learn from your mistakes

All clan elders are there to help you. When you fail in an attack, you can request feedback from an elder to learn why it failed and how you can improve.


If you are in the clan, failing repeatedly, but do not invest the time to learn from your mistakes, this is not the clan you want to join.

Be serious

Maintaining a good clan is a mutual effort from members and leaders: we do expect everyone to take their efforts seriously. Use common sense and don't opt-in to war if you cannot use both attacks.

Use our toolset

We use various tools to support our clan, which are described below. Use these properly: especially with things such as ClashTools we need everyone to use it, otherwise it's useless.

The Toolset

GroupMe

We use GroupMe for communications and have several chat channels. Here we discuss clan wars, strategies, decision making and more important things like sharing pictures of cats.


Once you are accepted into the clan, ask for an invite.

ClashTools

We use ClashTools to manage our clan: we register wars here, people call targets which they like to attack, and the leaders keep track of statistics and progress of all players.

Once you are inside the clan, sign up for an account at this site and join our clan.
